Alex J. DeGrave

Alex J. DeGrave is an MD/PhD student training at the University of Washington's Medical Scientist Training Program. He studies artificial intelligence in medicine, using state-of-the-art computational tools to understand how medical AIs make decisions and how these decisions can be prevented from going awry. He aims to ensure that medical AI is effective, safe, and of maximum benefit to patients and their providers.

Outside of work, he climbs, skis, and mountaineers in the Cascades range of Washington state.

Undergraduate education: B.S. Mathematics & Molecular Biology, University of Pittsburgh

PhD Program: Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science & Engineering

Advisor: Su-In Lee, PhD
